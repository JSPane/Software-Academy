Introduction

  • Stained Glass Polish is used to create a shiny and durable finish on solder seams.
  • It will assist in protecting against tarnish for a professional finish.
  • It can also restore a brilliant shine to already tarnished finishes.

How to Use

  1. Apply a small amount and spread a thin coat evenly over glass and/or metal.
  2. Rub lightly along seams to ensure a bright, even finish.
  3. Let dry to haze and buff to a high shine.
  4. A hair dryer can accelerate the drying process in humid or cold conditions.
  5. You're done! Feel free to show your friends!
